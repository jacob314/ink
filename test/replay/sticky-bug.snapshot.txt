This is a demo showing a scrollable box with sticky headers.
Press up/down arrow or w/s to scroll vertically (w/s for 30 lines, Shift for 10).
Press 'space' to add a block, 'c' to clear list.
Press 'b' to toggle scrollbar (on), 't' to toggle border.
Press 'f' to collapse footer, 'a' to toggle alternate buffer + sticky headers (current: off)
Press 'h' to toggle sticky headers in backbuffer (current: off)
Press 'v' to toggle stable scrollback (on)
